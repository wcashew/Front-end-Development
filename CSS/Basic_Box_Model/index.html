width

  • 指定 content box 宽度
  • 取值为长度、百分数、auto
  • auto 由浏览器根据其它属性确定
  • 百分数相对于容器的 content box 宽度

height

  • 指定 content box 高度
  • 取值为长度、百分数、auto
  • auto 由内容计算得来
  • 百分数相对于容器的 content box 高度
  • 容器有指定的高度时，百分数才生效

padding

  • 指定元素四个方向的内边距
  • 百分数相对于容器宽度

border

  • 指定容器边框样式、粗细和颜色

margin

  • 指定元素四个方向的外边距
  • 取值可以是长度、百分数和auto
  • 百分数相对于容器宽度

The common starling is a mediumsized perching bird in the starling family, Sturnidae. It is about 20 cm (8 in) long and has glossy black plumage, which is speckled with white at some times of year. The legs are pink and the bill is black in winter and yellow in summer; young birds have browner plumage than the adults. It is a noisy bird, especially in communal roosts, with an unmusical but varied song.
